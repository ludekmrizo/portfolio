Music

I am an active musician with more than twenty five years of experience. I graduated from the Conservatory and the Musical School of Arts in Ostrava, where I received professional music educated. I have been playing in a band that is a place for me for a long time. In addition, I jump in the Májovák orchestra, which allows me to get to know new music styles and experience. I also cooperate with the elementary art school in Karviná, where I play in different groups with younger generations. Music is inherently part of my life.

Climbing

The mountains are a place of peace and well -being for me. I am most attracted by the Beskydy Mountains, especially their Silesian part, which gives me amazing views and a sense of connection with nature. In my free time I like to go hikes, where I find not only physical activity, but above all a form of mental cleansing. Every hike allows me for a while to forget everyday worries and recharge new energy. For me, the mountains are a place where I can freely breathe and be myself.

Martial arts

For me, martial arts are more than just sport - they are the way to physical and mental development. I have experience with the Israeli Krav Maga, the Russian Systema and I am currently engaged in kickboxing. These disciplines help me get rid of stress, strengthen the body and gain confidence. I believe that martial arts domination brings not only skills but also responsibility. My motto is, "I do it so I don't have to use it." Combat sports teaches me discipline, respect and harmony of body and mind.
